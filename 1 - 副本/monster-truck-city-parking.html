Monster Truck City Parking

Monster Truck City Parking is a driving game where you follow the pink halos with your giant truck and go to the parking area. You must park your vehicle in the area before your time is up. When you reach the parking lot, gently get your vehicle in. The real challenge in this one of the most enjoyable parking games is properly fitting the huge vehicle into the parking space!
